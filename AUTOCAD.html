WHAT IS AUTO CAD

During my time in FabLab, I explored Fusion 360, a professional 3D design software developed by Autodesk.Fusion 360 is widely used in engineering, product design, and manufacturing because it combines CAD (Computer-Aided Design), CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing), and CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering) in a single platform. The main objective of this module was to learn the basics of 3D design step by step, starting from small sketches to building a complete project – in my case, a drone design.

Day 1

  • Learn how to download, install, and set up Fusion 360.
  • Understand the interface and tools.
  • Practice creating 2D sketches with constraints and dimensions.
  • Explore 3D features like extrude, revolve, sweep, loft.
  • Learn assembly and joints for combining parts.
  • Work on a final project (Drone design).
    • Fusion 360 is widely used for product design, automotive, aerospace, and robotics.
    Skills learned can be applied to 3D printing, CNC machining, and rapid prototyping.
    The drone design can be further developed into a real prototype.
